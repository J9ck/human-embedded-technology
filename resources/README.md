## üìÑ Key Papers

### BCI Landmarks
- Vidal (1973): "Toward Direct Brain-Computer Communication" - First BCI concept
- Wolpaw et al. (2002): "Brain-Computer Interfaces for Communication and Control"
- Hochberg et al. (2006): "Neuronal Ensemble Control of Prosthetic Devices" - BrainGate

### Deep Learning for Biosignals
- Lawhern et al. (2018): "EEGNet: A Compact CNN for EEG-based BCIs"
- Schirrmeister et al. (2017): "Deep Learning with Convolutional Neural Networks for EEG Decoding"

### Neural Interfaces
- Maynard et al. (1997): "The Utah Intracortical Electrode Array"
- Oxley et al. (2016): "Minimally Invasive Endovascular Stent-Electrode Array" - Stentrode
